HIGH SCHOOL MUSICAL ’Fits & Hits

January 1, 2021 by Jennie

“What team? Wildcats!” From the moment it aired on the Disney Channel on January 20, 2006, the High School Musical started a phenomenon that captured the hearts of millions around the world. In the first movie set at East High School, a group of students discover the power of music, friendship and self-expression. The memorable show tunes and storyline along with the appealing cast resonated deeply with audiences of all ages.

The opening scene of High School Musical takes place at a winter ski resort where we’re introduced to popular basketball player Troy Bolton (played by Zac Efron) and shy bookworm Gabriella Montez (played by Vanessa Anne Hudgens). Troy and Gabriella unexpectedly find themselves on stage at a New Year’s Eve party to sing a duet. Despite initial nervousness, they gain confidence and deliver a captivating performance, showcasing their talent and chemistry.
